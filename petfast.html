PetFast - Shopify Customization & Feature Development

PetFast is a Shopify-based online pet shop where we handled deep theme customization and developed unique features to make the store truly stand out. Our work focused on performance, interactivity, and giving customers a seamless, engaging shopping experience.

We enhanced the site’s design through custom-coded Shopify blocks, integrated a “Notify Me” feature for out-of-stock products, and added a smooth custom animation on the Fresh Pack page to showcase products with personality and motion.

With PetFast, we:

  • Customized the Shopify theme code to improve layout and user flow.
  • Built custom Shopify blocks to support new content and dynamic sections.
  • Developed the “Notify Me” functionality, giving customers instant updates when products are back in stock.
  • Designed and implemented custom animations for a modern, branded experience.

Through precision coding and creative design, we helped PetFast evolve beyond a standard Shopify template - turning it into a store that feels alive, polished, and uniquely theirs.
