PharouhFrog - Pet Grooming Management System

PharouhFrog is a fully custom-coded management system built to simplify and automate pet grooming operations. Designed for modern pet salons, it helps track every grooming session with ease, automatically calculates each groomer’s commission, and strengthens customer retention by keeping detailed visit histories.

Customers only need to enter their information once — future appointments are instantly recognized for a smooth, personalized experience.

With PharouhFrog, you can:

  • Search clients instantly by phone and view their full session history.
  • Filter sessions by date for quick insights and reporting.
  • Monitor waiting lines across multiple branches in real time.

Built from scratch with performance, simplicity, and scalability in mind — PharouhFrog turns everyday grooming chaos into organized flow.
